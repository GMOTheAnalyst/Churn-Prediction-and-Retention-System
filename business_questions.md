____Customer Churn & Retention Intelliegence System_____

_____Project Purpose______ ->

Understand why customers leave and identify those at risk, to enable targeted retention efforts that protect revenue and increase lifetime customer value.



	 _____Core Business Questions____ ->

1. Who has churned, who is active, and who is at risk of churning?  
   - Define churn using business rules (e.g., no purchases in last 90 days).  
   - Segment customers into these groups.

2. What is the monthly churn rate, and how does it trend over time? 
   - Calculate the percentage of customers lost each month.  
   - Identify seasonal or campaign impacts.

3. Which customers generate the most revenue, and how does churn affect revenue?
   - Lifetime value (LTV) per customer. (compute) 
   - Assess revenue at risk due to churn.

4. Which high-value customers are at highest risk of churn? 
   - Combine revenue and inactivity data to prioritize retention.

5. What is the retention rate month over month?**  
   - Track returning customers and retention improvement opportunities.


	 _______Why These Questions Matter_______ ->
- Customer retention is cheaper than acquisition.
- Understanding churn patterns helps tailor marketing and service strategies.  
- Protecting high-value customers maximizes business impact.


 _____Why These Questions Matter_______ ->
- Reducing churn increases revenue without acquiring new customers.
- Identifying at-risk customers allows for targeted retention campaigns.
- High-value customers at risk of churn need prioritized attention.
